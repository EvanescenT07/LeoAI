Prompt: can you explain me about Google 
******************

Google is a multinational technology company that specializes in internet-related services and products. It was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford University in California. Google is best known for its search engine, which dominates the market and is used by billions of people around the world to find information online.

In addition to its search engine, Google offers a wide range of products and services including advertising services, cloud computing, software applications, and hardware devices. Some of Google's most popular products include Gmail, Google Maps, Google Drive, YouTube, and the Android operating system.

Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. The company is known for its innovative and forward-thinking approach to technology, as well as its commitment to making the internet a more open and accessible place for all users.
